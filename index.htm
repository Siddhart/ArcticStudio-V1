Discord Bots


Free Games Bot

The Free Games Bot will notify your server when there is a new free game


Star Bot

Star Bot is a bot that users can use to give people stars for appreciation



Weekly Art Jams Bot

The Weekly Art Jams Bot will host weekly art jams in your Discord server. It will display a new theme every Monday at 12:00 CEST



Superhero Wiki

A bot which will give you some information about your favorite comic superheroes


Rocket Launch Tracker

This bot will notify the users in your server when a new rocket launch is announced


Hermes

This bot will send a random event that happened in the past on the same day
